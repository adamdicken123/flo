More Than Just Your Bank Records

don't have any accounting software?

Do it all in flo

Not everyone needs a full blown accounting solution, we totally get that and flo was born with that in mind.
We don't always need to predict the future if we know what's going to happen. That's why flo comes with a few extra tools to handle more than just bank records.

Sales Invoices

We will allow you to quickly generate and send out invoices to clients. This makes sure you keep a track of who owes you money and how that affects your cash flow. You can also set up alerts to help make sure you get paid.



Purchase Invoices

You can also input your invoices to keep a track of the things you need to pay, and you can see what happens to your cashflow depending on when you pay.

Anything Else

It's important to include everything when working out your cashflow. Thats why we make it easy for you to add in any other expected transactions - we'll even keep a track of if they happen or not, with. notifications if you want them.

don't duplicate your efforts

Connect to Accounting Software

We don't always need to predict the future, we can connect to your existing accounting software to bring in invoices and purchase orders to take some of the prediction out of the cash flow forecasting.

We plan to integrate with both Quickbooks Online & Xero through their API's.

We allow you to set up a range of notifications to help keep you in the know and in control of whats happening with your cash.

You can configure notifications for:

  • Starred debitors & creditors
  • New payees or suspicious looking payments
  • Custom expected transactions
  • Account balance limits

Be Alerted of Debtors

If you integrate your invoices we will look out for when they are or aren't paid. Get notifications as soon as an invoice is approaching the agreed credit terms and stay in command.

We will also produce a quick list of all outstanding invoices, all up to date because we know about payments as soon as they come in - no more waiting to do your accounts before you know how much you're owed. Why not get an email digest each day!

Look Ahead

Using advanced machine learning algorithms and statistical techniques we scan through all of your past finances, at a transaction level to come up with a sensible outlook for the future.

take control &

Adjust

Ok, so we can't always get things perfect. We predict sensible boundaries and dates for upcoming payments but it's easy to change these and watch how the results play out.

Understand

When we make a forecast of a transaction it is based on distilling the data and detecting trends. We surface these trends to you so you know what we're thinking and you get a better understanding of your finances.

Open Banking

put your feet up &

Relax

Flo uses new Open Banking technology to securely access your bank transactions. This means that all you need to do is grant access to flo to start getting instant smarts about your cash flow.

scanning the market

Get Smart with Recommended Products

stay notified &

Avoid Shortfalls

Flo sends notifications as soon as a potential future shortfall in your cashflow is detected. Even better, flo will suggest a solution to keep you on track.

Flo scans the market to find the right product for your situation. Since we know your cashflow we can recommend the right payment schedule to get you back on track whilst minimizing cost. You can test how the loan will impact your future cashflow at a click, and when you're ready to choose we do our best to minimise the friction between you and the provider.

look to the future &

Invest

Carrying cash might be wasting potential, when we detect that you're plain sailing we will recommend that you invest a suitable amount.

You could invest back into your business or choose from a range of products or existing savings you have. As with loans, flo will scan the market and evaluate your options for you. You also have the power to tweak your investment and see instantly how this will impact your future cashflow.

don't be taken advantage of &

Get the Best Accounts

We know how you use your bank accounts. Payments, overdrafts - each account has its own pros & cons. We analyse which accounts or combination of accounts will minimise the fees you pay, and how to use each one best.

However inputing details can be time consuming & not everyone needs a full blown accounting solution, we totally get that and that's why flo comes with a few extra tools to handle more than just bank records with ease.

Sales Invoices

Flo helps you to quickly generate and send out invoices to clients. This makes sure you keep a track of who owes you money and how that affects your cash flow. You can also set up alerts to help make sure you get paid. Let flo be your credit control team.



Purchase Invoices

You can input your invoices to keep a track of the things you need to pay, and you can see what happens to your cashflow depending on when you pay. In the future we plan to automate this process as much as possible by scanning email attachments and allowing you to upload images and pdfs - watch this space.



Anything Else

It's important to include everything when working out your cashflow. Thats why we make it easy for you to add in any other expected transactions - we'll even keep a track of if they happen or not, with notifications if you want them.

Connect to Accounting Software

If you are already digitally managing your bookkeeping we can connect to your existing accounting software to bring in invoices and purchase orders to take some of the prediction out of the cashflow forecasting.

We plan to integrate with both Quickbooks Online & Xero through their API's at launch.

Notifications

be in the know &

Get Updates On Your Money

Flo helps you to set up a range of notifications to help keep you in the know and in control of whats happening with your cash.

You can configure notifications for:

  • Starred debitors & creditors
  • New payees or suspicious looking payments
  • Custom expected transactions
  • Account balance limits either now or predicted

take control &

Be Alerted of Debtors

If you integrate your sales invoices, flo will look out for when they are or aren't paid. Get notifications as soon as an invoice is approaching the agreed credit terms and stay in command.

Flo will also produce a quick list of all outstanding invoices, all up to date because we know about payments as soon as they come in - no more waiting to do your accounts before you know how much you're owed. Why not get an email digest each day!

Flo can also take it one step further and take action on outstanding invoices, either automatically or after your approval. Let flo be your credit control team.
